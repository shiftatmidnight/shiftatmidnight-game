About The Game

Shift at Midnight is a horror simulation game where you work the midnight shift at a rural 90s gas station. Some of your customers are only pretending to be human. You can ask each customer only 5 questions, forcing you to examine their ID, cross-check details in the computer, and observe their behavior carefully.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shift at Midnight free to play?

Yes! A free single-player demo is currently available on our website. We're working towards 20,000 Steam wishlists to unlock a free multiplayer co-op demo. The full version will be a paid release on Steam.

How many players can play together?

Shift at Midnight supports 1-3 players in cooperative multiplayer. You can play solo or team up with up to 2 friends to work together during the night shift.

What makes this game unique?

The game combines Papers Please-style document verification with survival horror elements. The core mechanic of asking only 5 questions per customer creates strategic tension, while the co-op gameplay adds team coordination dynamics.
