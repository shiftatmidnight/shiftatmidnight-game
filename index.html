About The Game

Shift at Midnight is a horror simulation game where you work the midnight shift at a rural 90s gas station. Some of your customers are only pretending to be human. You can ask each customer only 5 questions, forcing you to examine their ID, cross-check details in the computer, and observe their behavior carefully.

Key Features

5-Question Investigation System

Limited question system creates tension, ID verification mechanics, Computer database cross-referencing, Behavioral pattern observation

Co-op Horror Experience

1-3 player cooperative gameplay, Shared investigation responsibilities, Team-based survival strategies

Defense Phase Survival

Barricade construction mechanics, Trap placement system, Hide-and-seek survival elements
